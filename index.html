WHAT IS PAPO?

Papo is a mobile application that will be used to rent out vehicles, from individuals or from car rental companies.

WHY INVEST?

Papo provides a unique investment opportunity that will serve to fill on of the few gaps still present within the Kenyan digital market. At present there are no applications available that faithfully fulfill this role in the Kenyan digital market public consciousness.

WHAT’S DIFFERENT?

Many an individual can create an application similar to Papo, however, the Papo application, as a result of its unique approach to user security, will provide assurance to the renters through a proprietary approach to car renting in the digital space.
